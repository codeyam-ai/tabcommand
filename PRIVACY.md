# TabCommand Privacy Policy

The canonical TabCommand privacy policy lives at:

## **https://codeyam.com/tabcommand/privacy**

That page is the version submitted to the Chrome Web Store. It is the single
source of truth — this file intentionally does not duplicate it, so the two
cannot drift apart.

## The short version

**TabCommand collects no data.** Your tabs, groups, labels, favorites, history,
and settings are stored only on your own device using Chrome's local extension
storage (`chrome.storage.local`). Nothing is transmitted to us or to any third
party. There is no analytics, no telemetry, and no tracking.

TabCommand reads tab titles and URLs solely to display, search, and organize
them for you, locally. It does not read page content.
